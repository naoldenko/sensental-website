we aim to help elite athletes who suffer from depression and/or anxiety live better lives

according to a 2015 study released by the ncaa, 30% of athletes have reported being overwhelmed as a collegiate athlete; we believe this is a rising issue, and hope to solve this problem

to solve this problem, we’re creating support tools for elite athletes by using artificial intelligence and therapeutic technique research

we’re currently in R&D mode and hope to launch a private beta late-2021
